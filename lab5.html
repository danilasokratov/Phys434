PT is the transverse (xy) momentum, given by pt = sqrt(px^2 + py^2), where px and py are the momenta along x and y.

EE2 is the 2-point ECF ratio, given by sum over i<j in J of (p_t,i * p_t,j * deltaR_ij * 1/(p_t,J)^2)
