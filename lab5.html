Sort the data into individual vectors. "h" prefixes are for higgs data, "q" prefixes are for background data.
hPT = higgsData(1,:);
qPT = qcdData(1,:);
PT is the transverse (xy) momentum, given by pt = sqrt(px^2 + py^2), where px and py are the momenta along x and y.
hETA = higgsData(2,:);
qETA = qcdData(2,:);
ETA is the pseudorapidity, given by -ln(tan(Ã¸/2))-0.5ln((abs(p)+pl)/(abs(p)-pl))

PHI is the azimuthal angle, given by arccos(x/r)
hMASS = higgsData(4,:);
qMASS = qcdData(4,:);
MASS is the mass invariant, given by E^2 = p^2 + m^2 (natural units).
hEE2 = higgsData(5,:);
qEE2 = qcdData(5,:);
EE2 is the 2-point ECF ratio, given by sum over i<j in J of (p_t,i * p_t,j * deltaR_ij * 1/(p_t,J)^2)
hEE3 = higgsData(6,:);
qEE3 = qcdData(6,:);
EE3 is the 3-point ECF ratio, given by sum over i<j<k in J of (p_t,i * p_t,j * p_t,k * deltaR_ij * deltaR_ik * deltaR_jk * 1/(p_t,J)^3)
hD2 = higgsData(7,:);
qD2 = qcdData(7,:);
D2 is the 3-to-2 point ECF ratio, given by EE3/(EE2)^3
